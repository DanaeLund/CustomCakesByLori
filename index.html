Our Story

My passion for cake decorating began with my oldest daughter's first birthday. I crafted a "healthy" carrot cake shaped like a ladybug. Though my daughter didn’t take a bite, this quirky creation sparked my love for cake artistry. Each year, I eagerly anticipated designing a special cake for my kids, challenging myself to improve with each one.

Recently, I started taking orders, transforming my passion into a business. I find immense joy in combining my baking skills with my fine art background. Trained in printmaking and studio arts at Emily Carr Institute of Art and Design, and with eight years of experience at Terra Breads in Vancouver, I bring both creativity and expertise to every cake I make.

Let me create something amazing for your special occasion!
